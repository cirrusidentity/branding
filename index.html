White Logo

The white version of The Cirrus Identity logo may be presented on non-light colored backgrounds. Examples of how the white The Cirrus Identity logo can be used:

  • over a full color photo (the portion of the photo the logo is placed over cannot obscure the any part of the logo)
  • over a solid color background (the color must be dark enough to easily see all parts of the logo)
  • Layered on top of a gradient (the gradient must be dark enough to easily see all parts of the logo)

Colors

#579FD1

Light Blue

HEX: #579FD1
RGB: 87 159 209
CMYK: 64 25 3 0
PANTONE: 542 C

Light blue can be used for main headings. It can also be used in gradients, solid color backgrounds, and other supporting graphical elements.

#2B6186

Dark Blue

HEX: #2B6186
RGB: 43 97 134
CMYK: 88 59 29 9

Dark blue can be used for less prominent headings. It can also be used in gradients, solid color backgrounds, and other supporting graphical elements.

#A5C8DF

Pale Blue

HEX: #A5C8DF
RGB: 165 200 223
CMYK: 34 11 6 0

#b7b7b7

Light Gray

HEX: #b7b7b7
RGB: 183 183 183

Light gray can be used for quotation credits and other text that should be less prominent.

Gray

Dark Gray

#8BC646

Green

RGB: 139 198 70
